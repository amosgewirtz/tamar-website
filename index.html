I am a doctoral candidate in Economics at Northwestern University. My research focuses on macroeconomics, monetary policy, and international economics.

Before Northwestern, I received my M.Sc. in Economics from the Stockholm School of Economics, where I graduated first in my class. I also hold a B.Sc. (Hons) in Psychology, Politics, Law and Economics from the University of Amsterdam, graduating summa cum laude.

I have worked in the International Policy Analysis department at the European Central Bank and in the Monetary Policy department at De Nederlandsche Bank.

Research Interests

Macroeconomics, monetary policy, international economics, trade policy, inequality.
